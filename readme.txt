== Description ==

FT QuickLogin is a simple and versatile plugin built to enhance the whole Signup and Login process by displaying all authentification forms in a lightbox.

Features include:

* Choose between two layout styles: bright and dark
* Easily upload your own logo to be displayed in the lightbox.
* Select the type of Posts to be displayed on the top bar: Latest posts, Popular posts, Random posts or Sticky posts.
* Choose whether to hide or show the QuickLogin bar after the user is logged in.
* Easily customize the Primary and Secondary(hover) text colors of the QuickLogin bar.

To test drive QuickLogin [click here](http://testdrive.flarewp.com/quicklogin/)

== Installation ==

1. Install FT QuickLogin either via the WordPress.org plugin directory, or by uploading the files to your server
2. After activating FT QuickLogin head over to the settings page(Settings -> QuickLogin).
3. That's it.  You're good to go!

== Frequently Asked Questions ==

= How can I choose the lightbox color? =

Choose the color scheme that's right for your blog (for a bright layout choose Light, for a darker one choose Dark)

= How can I upload my logo? =

To upload your logo simply click the Upload Image button and browse your logo. Alternatively you can enter your logo URL.

= I Activated the plugin but the Top Bar isn't showing up =

By default the Top Bar is disabled, to enable it tick the Enable Top Bar box.

= What is the "Content Width"? =

The content Width is the absolute with of the design of your blog.

= What is the News Section? =

The News Section appears on the left side of the Top Bar, where you can set which types of posts to be displayed and rotated.

= How can I customize the Top Bar's font colors? =

Simply enter the hex codes for the Primary (base) color and the Secondary(hover) color.
